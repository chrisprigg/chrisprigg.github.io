WHY HITLER? - Spring 2021 - Final Project

by CHRIS PRIGG

·

For this final project I have decided to create a podcast giving a glimpse into the AfD or Alternatives for Germany. Founded in 2013, the AfD is a far-right political party in Germany that has gained traction in recent years due to its outward opposition to immigration and strong stance on keeping Germany as German as possible. With this, I take some of these ideas and relate them back to topics and conversations we discussed in class revolving around Hitler and the Nazi party during its rise around World War II.

Propaganda #1 Nazi - Image used by the Nazi party during their rule of Germany to spark hatred for the Jews within their communities. In addition, this image blames the Jews for the war. The poster reads, “The Jew: The inciter of war, the prolonger of war”

Propaganda #1 AfD - Billboard used by the AfD to encourage the stopping of immigration into Germany. The billboard reads, “Stop Islamization”

Propaganda #2 Nazi - Image used by the Nazi party during their rule of Germany to encourage German women to give birth to strong German children that will carry the Nazi ideals in their life. The poster reads, “Support the assistance program for mothers and children”

Propaganda #2 AfD - Poster used by AfD to encourage German women to give birth and raise children in Germany with the values that align with the party. The poster reads, “New Germans? We’ll make them ourselves."
